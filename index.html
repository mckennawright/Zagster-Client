ABOUT THE DATA AND ZAGSTER

This Data is About Zagster Bike use from 2017-2018. What is Zagster?

At Zagster, our mission is to make the bike the most loved form of transportation. And we’re doing this by bringing our proven bike sharing platform to communities across North America.

"There’s a revolution going on in urban mobility. People are taking control of how they get around town by rediscovering the joy, freedom and empowerment of bikes." A majority of people, including two-thirds of young adults, want to live and work where they won’t often need personal cars. And though most say they want to bike more often, only half have a bike. These transportation shifts create a ripe environment for bike sharing to flourish in communities of all types and sizes.

From Zagster Site, Written By Founder: Timothy Ericson
